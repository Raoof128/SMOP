# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

## Expected Behavior
- Be kind, inclusive, and professional.
- Assume good intent and offer constructive feedback.
- Respect privacy and follow security best practices.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Any conduct that disrupts collaboration or violates applicable laws.

## Reporting
- Report incidents to the maintainers via issue or email (no sensitive data in reports).
- All reports are reviewed promptly; retaliation is prohibited.

## Enforcement
- Maintainers may take appropriate action including warnings, temporary bans, or removal from the project.
- Decisions prioritize community safety and project security.
